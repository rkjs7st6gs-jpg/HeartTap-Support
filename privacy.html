Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Heart Tap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All heart rate data and app settings are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted off the device and is not accessible by the developer.

In-app purchases are handled securely by Apple. The developer does not receive or store any payment information.
