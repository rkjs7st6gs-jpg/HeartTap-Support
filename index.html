What Heart Tap does

Heart Tap lets you track heart rate using taps. Tap along with your heartbeat and the app will calculate your heart rate in real time.

How to use

  1. Tap the heart icon in time with your heartbeat.
  2. Heart Tap will show your live heart rate.
  3. Upgrade to Pro to save readings and customize settings.

Pro Features

  • Save heart rate readings for later
  • Customize app colors and settings
